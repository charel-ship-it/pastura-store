🌿 What is Pastura?

Pastura is a modern digital platform dedicated to providing essential tools and equipment for farmers and agricultural enthusiasts. The platform simplifies access to high-quality, practical farming supplies, empowering both small-scale and professional agricultural operations through seamless online shopping.

  • Industry Focus: Tools for poultry, gardening, irrigation, and sustainable farming.
  • Audience: Hobbyists, small farms, livestock keepers, and home growers.
  • Features: Secure Shopify shopping, Firebase authentication, mobile optimization.

“Pastura is your go-to online hub for modern farming and gardening supplies — empowering your farm with smart, practical tools that get the job done.”
